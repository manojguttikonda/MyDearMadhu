The Ride (Ottawa to Toronto)

How It All Began

We met in a cab from Ottawa to Toronto. My friends and I were loudly chatting and using some Telugu words, completely oblivious. Then I saw you smiling, Madhu, and I couldn't help but ask, "Where are you from?" You smiled and said, "I am from your place." The connection was instant!

The Challenge & Rave

Changing Your Mind

You hated Canada and were planning to return to Cary, NC in a month. I confidently told you in that cab, "I will change your opinion, Madhu." That very night, we went to a rave party together. Even though the party was a letdown, it was the start of something magical.

Friend's Birthday

The First Kiss

After days of non-stop chatting, we met up at our friend's birthday party. The tension was real, and I gathered up the courage to ask, "Can I kiss you, Madhu?" From that moment, we opened our hearts. Our friend groups merged into one big family, and the partying never stopped!

USA Move (A New Chapter)

No Distance Can Separate Us

The day came for you to move to the USA. Standing there, you couldn't bear to leave, admitting that your opinion of Canada had truly changed because of us. Now, even with the distance, we happily meet every single month. Manoj and Madhu, together forever, and no distance can come between us.
